Code of Conduct

Rules and Regulations

  1. All the team members will mandatorily be the girls of MIET.
  2. Participating team will constitute of maximum 3 members.
  3. Timings for the Hackathon.- 10 am start on Day 1 and it continues overnight (students are free to be at the campus or outside but need to work on it overnight.) It ends at 4 pm on Day 2.
  4. One Technical Mentor (Male/Female) is allowed to mentor the team.
  5. Broad Application Area, Problem Statements and details of Development Platform will be shared beforehand.
  6. Expected Outcome(s) will be shared on the spot, once a team selects a particular problem statement
  7. Not more than two teams will be allowed to work on same problem statement at a time.
  8. Teams can choose and work upon more than one problem statements only once they have completed the previously allotted one
  9. Request to change the Problem Statement, after allocation, will be on sole discretion of the organization committee
  10. Accommodation will be provided to teams based upon availability in Guest House and Hostels
  11. On-spot mentoring will also be provided by the organizing Institute by a panel of Mentors. Participants can reach out to this panel in case of need.
    12. Notes
    • For participating in this Hackathon only core programming skills are required.
    • You have to work in day and night both , in night you can work from home or from college.

Problem Statements

A new way to deliver business applications

Solving business problems: that is the main role of technology. And in an ideal world, technology would deliver business solutions – and do it fast . But because most organizations have set up IT infrastructure primarily to automate, standardize, and streamline processes enterprise-wide, personalizing applications for each user can be an elusive goal.

SAP Cloud Platform is an open platform as a service that plays an indispensable role as the innovation core of an intelligent enterprise. It modernizes and connects with your core business technologies and processes to create digital experiences enriched with intelligent technologies such as machine learning. In business speak, it is a means by which to personalize applications for individual priorities and integrate them with core enterprise applications. In short, it’s a way to solve individual business problems within an intelligent enterprise, empower your employees, engage your customers – and run better. In its indispensable role in delivering accelerated and trusted innovation for transformative busi-ness outcomes, SAP Cloud Platform can help you:

  • Empower lines of business with applications for every role and department and personalize core applications
  • Build new and extend existing applications more easily and economically
  • Deliver open, agile, flexible applications that run on any browser and device by using simple and modern design principles, employing the skills you already have
  • Safely integrate business assets, the IoT, social networks, collaboration tools, business networks, and diverse customer interaction channels
  • Connect on-premise and cloud applications, from SAP S/4HANA and SAP Business Suite to software-as-a-service applications such as SAP SuccessFactors solutions, the SAP C/4HANA suite, and the SAP Analytics Cloud solution
  • Extend and personalize other on-premise and cloud applications – regardless of who built them – to maximize the value of existing investments
