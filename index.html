Code of Conduct

Rules and Regulations

  1. All the team members will mandatorily be the girls
  2. Participating team will constitute of maximum 3 members.
  3. Maximum 3 Teams from an Institution will be allowed
  4. One Technical Mentor (Male/Female) is allowed to mentor the team.
  5. Broad Application Area, Problem Statements and details of Development Platform will be shared beforehand
  6. Expected Outcome(s) will be shared on the spot, once a team selects a particular problem statement
  7. Not more than two teams will be allowed to work on same problem statement at a time
  8. Teams can choose and work upon more than one problem statements only once they have completed the previously allotted one
  9. Request to change the Problem Statement, after allocation, will be on sole discretion of the organization committee
  10. Accommodation will be provided to outstation teams based upon availability in Guest House and Hostels
  11. Registration fee for the event will be ₹ 500 per participating team.
  12. On-spot mentoring will also be provided by the organizing Institute by a panel of Mentors. Participants can reach out to this panel in case of need.
  13. Winning team will be announced, as per the recommendations of Jury panel, on the evening of 10th October. Winners will be get prize money as:
    • Winner - ₹15000
    • 1st Runner-up - ₹7000
    • 2nd Runner-up - ₹5000

1: DISCRETE MANUFACTURING INDUSTRY FOR MAKE-TO-ORDER PRODUCTION STRATEGY. SAP S/4HANA , SAP Fiori
Description

In a Make-to-Order scenario, once Sales Orders are received from a customer, subsequent Planned Orders and Production Orders are generated against it. It may happen that in the meanwhile, another Sales Order for the same product and/or spares with same configuration is received from a Premium customer. In such a scenario, the Sales agents need to have the flexibility to divert an existing order to the premium customer to enable faster delivery to him. To do so, a Sales agent taking the order needs to check beforehand if there is any product already in pipeline for another Sales Order. If yes, the agent can commit a shorter delivery date and create a Sales Order with the same.


2: F1 RACING SAP fiori , SAP Predictive Analytics
Description

F1 race drivers have to take critical decision of stopping at the pit for repairs and part replacements during a race. This decision sets them back a few critical seconds but it is important to have the car in good condition to run optimally till the finish line. The vital stats that must be available to the driver at all times are as follows:
• Current Speed
• Fuel left & Tank Capacity
• Tire condition (How many km it can run without tire wearing off)
• Engine Heat
• Next Pit distance
• Distance to Finish line
• Gap between the driver and race leader, etc.
It is imperative for a driver to take this tough decision multiple times during a single race. Correct decision making can prove to be the difference between winning and losing.
